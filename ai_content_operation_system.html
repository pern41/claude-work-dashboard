Operational Diagnosis

The main bottleneck is not creativity. It is operational consistency. Every recurring output should have a defined input, owner, status, template, quality check, and measurement loop.

  • AI summary posts depend on manual curation and writing.
  • Handout projects lack a repeatable brief-to-delivery process.
  • Comment replies consume time because there is no triage system.
  • Video planning is disconnected from performance feedback.
  • Content decisions are not yet strongly data-driven.

SOP Structure

SOPs should be short, modular, and attached to templates. Each SOP defines the owner, inputs, steps, output standard, QA checklist, and automation trigger.

Standards
01SOP

Idea Intake

  • Capture source, topic, audience pain, and format.
  • AI classifies topic cluster and suggests angle.
  • Human approves strategic fit.
02SOP

Research and Brief

  • Collect source links and factual claims.
  • AI creates summary, risks, examples, and outline.
  • Research owner verifies claims.
03SOP

Script and Handout

  • Use script, post, and handout templates.
  • AI drafts first version and CTA variants.
  • Creator adds voice, examples, and judgment.
04SOP

Publishing

  • Check title, description, thumbnail, link, CTA.
  • AI suggests metadata and channel snippets.
  • Ops schedules assets and logs publish date.
05SOP

Community

  • Tag comments by intent and sentiment.
  • AI drafts replies and detects lead intent.
  • Human approves sensitive replies.
06SOP

Analytics

  • Review weekly dashboard every Monday.
  • AI identifies winners, weak spots, and patterns.
  • Strategist updates next-week priorities.

KPI System

Measure production health, content quality, audience response, and business outcomes. Avoid making decisions from views alone.

Measurement

Production KPIs

  • Assets shipped per week.
  • Cycle time by content type.
  • Overdue tasks and blockers.
  • Revision count and QA pass rate.

Content KPIs

  • CTR and packaging performance.
  • Retention and average view duration.
  • Engagement rate, comments, shares, saves.
  • Topic cluster performance.

Business KPIs

  • Handout downloads and opt-ins.
  • CTA click rate and lead conversion.
  • Email or CRM list growth.
  • Qualified lead comments and DMs.

Content Score Formula

Content Score =
30% Retention quality
20% CTR / hook strength
20% Engagement rate
20% Lead conversion
10% Strategic fit

Weekly Analytics Review

  • Pull dashboard every Monday.
  • Identify top 3 winners and bottom 3 weak assets.
  • Tag reasons: topic, hook, timing, packaging, CTA, retention.
  • Extract 3 audience insights from comments.
  • Update backlog scores and next-week plan.

Risks and Bottlenecks

The most likely failure mode is automating a messy process. Stabilize the system before scaling output or adding complex automations.

Controls

Process Risk

  • Too many statuses or fields make the system heavy.
  • Unclear owners cause missed handoffs.
  • Folder chaos leads to repeated work.

AI Risk

  • Generic drafts reduce brand voice.
  • Incorrect claims damage trust.
  • Auto-replies can mishandle sensitive comments.

Data Risk

  • Tracking too many metrics creates confusion.
  • Views can distract from retention and leads.
  • No review rhythm means no learning loop.

Mitigation

  • Use simple v1 systems and improve monthly.
  • Keep human approval for claims and sensitive replies.
  • Review KPIs weekly and templates monthly.

Scalability Recommendations

Scale by increasing repeatability, not by adding more manual effort. The backend should make every status change trigger the next expected action.

Scale Logic

Backend Operation Logic

Each content item must have:
- Unique content ID
- Topic cluster
- Funnel stage
- Owner
- Status
- Due date
- Source links
- Asset folder
- AI prompts used
- Publish channels
- CTA / lead magnet
- Performance metrics
- Decision after review

Every status change should trigger:
- Next task assignment
- Required template
- Due date update
- Blocker notification
- Dashboard update

Scale Rules

  • Do not scale volume until 2 videos per week is predictable.
  • Create one template before delegating any recurring task.
  • Track by topic cluster and funnel stage, not only platform.
  • Use AI for drafts, classification, summaries, and patterns.
  • Keep final creative judgment and factual approval human-owned.
  • Turn winners into series, lead magnets, and product ideas.
