Executive Summary

MED is a company health page designed to build brand presence online, educate audiences, and keep the business visible across social media through short, understandable, practical health content.

Strategic Core

What MED Should Become

MED should not remain only a content posting page. The stronger long-term model is a Health Media System that creates traffic, trust, community, leads, and revenue while remaining operational even when team members change.

  • Publish practical health knowledge from credible sources.
  • Use short-form social content for reach and sharing.
  • Use website SEO for durable organic traffic.
  • Use LINE OA and community loops for retention and recruitment.

SWOT Matrix

The strategy should protect credibility while using speed, AI workflow, visual quality, and multi-channel distribution to grow faster than traditional health media.

Decision Matrix

Strengths

  • Can produce many content formats across social and website.
  • AI can accelerate research, drafting, captions, and workflow.
  • SEO and social can grow together instead of separately.
  • Content pipeline can become a repeatable operating system.

Weaknesses

  • Brand awareness is still low and must be built from zero.
  • Community is not yet strong enough for loyalty effects.
  • Trust takes time, consistency, and source discipline.
  • Team changes require SOPs, prompt files, and handoff systems.

Opportunities

  • Health and wellness demand keeps growing in Thailand.
  • Short-form and shareable practical tips still have upside.
  • Google rewards helpful health content when it is useful and reliable.
  • Brands still value trusted publishers even in the AI content era.

Threats

  • Competition is high and audience attention is fragmented.
  • Platform algorithms can change without warning.
  • AI-generated spam can reduce trust in health content broadly.
  • Wrong health information can damage credibility quickly.

90-Day Action Plan

The first 90 days should install the content operating system, test high-performing formats, and prepare the brand assets needed for SEO, community growth, and future sponsor outreach.

Execution
Days 1-30

Setup & Foundation

Build the base: branding, SOP, templates, content pillars, website readiness, and first SEO articles.

  • Define mood, tone, source rules, and visual identity.
  • Create SOP, prompt file, content calendar, and template pack.
  • Open or optimize Facebook, TikTok, Instagram, Website, LINE OA.
  • Publish first 20 SEO articles and daily social content.
Days 31-60

Growth & Validation

Push reach and engagement while discovering the most repeatable formats, hooks, and audience pain points.

  • Post Reels and TikTok daily with multiple hook styles.
  • Share content into relevant communities and answer comments.
  • Run weekly analytics reviews to identify top topics.
  • Start creator collaboration and content format experiments.
Days 61-90

Scale & Sponsor Prep

Turn early audience data into a sponsor-ready growth story while deepening SEO and community retention.

  • Build media kit with audience, reach, engagement, and sample formats.
  • Create lead capture loops through LINE OA and website CTAs.
  • Publish evergreen SEO clusters and internal links.
  • Prepare first sponsored content package and partnership list.
